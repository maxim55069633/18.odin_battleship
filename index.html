Your grid

Drag your ships to your grid. Only drag the topmost(for a vertical ship) or leftmost(for a horizontal ship) square of the ship will work.

The ships cannot overlap.

You can't place a ship next to another.

Opponent's grid

Players alternate turns calling "shots" at the other player's ships (Click on a single square). Every time a player sinks his opponent's ship, he can shoot one more time. When a player sinks all ships his opponent has, he wins.

Every square has four states: 1. unknown place(blank square), 2. missed shot(interpunct), 3. hidden ship(blue square), 4. sunk ship(red square with cross)
